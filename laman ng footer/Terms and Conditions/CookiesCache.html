BookQuest uses cookies and cache to enhance your browsing experience. This section explains what cookies and cache are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

  1. What Are Cookies?

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience on our site.

  2. How We Use Cookies

    We use cookies to:

    • Remember your login information.
    • Track your usage of our site to improve our services.
    • Provide personalized content and recommendations.

  3. What Is Cache?

    Cache is temporary storage on your device that helps speed up the loading of web pages by storing parts of them locally.
